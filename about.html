About us

First Labels has been operating successfully since 1996. Our mission is to be the most efficient and cost-effective specialists in Label Manufacturing, surpassing the expectations of our clients. We are committed to promoting environmental well-being while achieving prosperity for our company and our people.

Our core beliefs are as follows:

  • Clients First: We prioritize the needs and satisfaction of our clients.
  • People as Assets: We recognize that our greatest asset is our talented team.
  • Team Efforts for Growth: Through collaborative efforts, we aim for profitable growth.
  • Constant Improvement: We strive for defect-free operations by continuously improving productivity and quality through training and open communication.
  • Strong Marketing: We have a robust marketing program in place to secure our future success.
  • Enjoyable and Rewarding Work: We believe that work should always be enjoyable and rewarding.

At First Labels, we provide comprehensive label services throughout Southern Africa. With branches countrywide, we ensure that we meet the labeling needs of Southern Africa effectively.

No job is too big or too small for us, and our unwavering commitment to improving our service and quality through our ISO quality system makes us the perfect business partner for all your label requirements.
