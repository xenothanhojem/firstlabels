Our Services

Custom Labels

At our label manufacturing company, we utilize a range of advanced methods such as flexo and digital printing to produce high-quality labels. Our extensive clientele spans across diverse industries, including agriculture, baking, beverage, confectionery, cosmetics, engineering, food, fruit and vegetable, health and nutraceutical, household chemicals, industrial chemicals, motor, sauces, spices, stationery, toiletries, wine, and many more. We take pride in serving a wide range of sectors, providing tailored labeling solutions to meet the unique requirements of each industry.

Flexo Labels

Labels printed using Photopolymer - Flexographic printing plates. We manufacture these in-house. We have a fully-functioning repro department that finalizes artwork for platemaking. Fully trained and experienced machine operators are able to meet your requirements. Many colours can be printed in Spot colours or with process colours to achieve impressive results. Labels manufactured with combinations gloss, matt, clear, lamination or varnish. We have hundreds of cutting dies with many different shapes and sizes. Printing can be done on various substrates - paper, polypropylene, and many more.

Advantages:

  • Print combinations of process and spot colours
  • Choose from thousands of cutting dies in stock
  • Cost effective larger print runs
  • Wide variety of substrates
  • HD plates that rival offset printing
  • Exceptional quality
  • Great for medium to large print runs

Digital Labels

Digital Labels printed using UV Inkjet Technology offering superb printing results. Full colour printing including white. 90 % of the Pantone Spectrum can be simulated using Cyan, Magenta, Yellow and Black (CMYK). We can achieve a beautiful floating result printed on Clear material using a Opaque White. We offer labels manufactured with combinations of gloss, matt, clear, laminated, varnished, foiled or a raised embossed feel. We have hundreds of cutting dies with many different shapes and sizes.

We can print onto many types of materials - paper, film & metalized.

Advantages:

  • This is a cost effective way to manufacture labels with smaller runs being possible.
  • No printing plate costs.
  • Any size or shape without cutting die costs can be manufactured.
  • Material is web fed, so labels are supplied on a roll which is ideal for machine applied labels.
  • Coatings like varnish or laminations are also offered.
  • Great for small to medium print runs

Stock Labels

Discover our wide range of stock labels available for immediate purchase. Choose from a selection of both blank and printed labels to meet your specific needs. Our printed labels include commonly used options such as Fragile labels, while our blank labels are ideal for printing variable information like barcodes and weights.

We maintain a comprehensive inventory of labels in various substrates and adhesives, including Thermal, Semi-Gloss, Polyprop, and more. If your desired label size is not currently in stock, don't worry! We have the capability to swiftly manufacture it for you.

If you're uncertain about the type of label that best suits your requirements, please don't hesitate to contact us. Our knowledgeable team is here to provide guidance and assist you in making the right choice.

Advantages of using blank labels and printing yourself?

  • You save money on origination and plate costs
  • You can have a vast range of products without having to have a large stock of many different labels.

Thermal Labels

When you need to labels on demand, direct thermal labels are affordable, easy-to-use solutions. We offer a wide range of blank roll labels designed for all makes and models of printers. These labels can be die cut to any custom size, in quantities from one hundred labels to hundreds of thousands. We also have stock ready made labels of the popular sizes. We can guide you to develop a custom label for your application if required. The thermal labels can be preprinted with a information generic information like a logo. Then you just overprint details required with you thermal printer.

Scale Labels

We offer a wide range of scale labels, generally used in butchery, bakery and fresh produce applications. We can manufacture blank and custom printed thermal scale labels of the highest quality using the best adhesives in various grades. We stock many of the popular brand scale labels too like SPAR, PnP etc. Economy scale labels are suitable for dry goods applications and have no barrier coating. Top coated thermal scale labels are suitable for moderate exposure to moisture, such as fresh produce. Double barrier coated thermal scale labels are suitable for high moisture applications such as frozen meats or seafood.

Linerless Labels

We offer linerless labels in standard sizes. These labels are ideal for goods that are packed in boxes, cardboard sleeves, plastic bags and more. Printing of codes (barcodes and QR codes) is Labels are now far better than the older linerless labels that constantly jammed printers. These labels adhere to any packaging materials in most conditions.

Did you know?

QR is short for Quick Response (they can be read quickly by a cell phone). Your business, no matter how small or large, could use QR codes in a number of ways. You might auto generate one containing all the product details, the number to call and the URL link to the page so they can show their friends on their cell phone. You could add one to any product containing your contact details so its easy for someone to add you to their contacts on their cell phone.

  • A link to your YouTube video
